About NERCO

Established in the year 1953, we Nerco Home Appliances are an ISI Certified Company, specializing in the manufacturing of Wall Fans, Table Fans, Ceiling Fans, Exhaust Fans, Coolers, Cooler Motors, Pedestal Fans, Room Heaters, Immersion Rods, and household appliances. Manufactured using supreme-in-class material and progressive tools & technology, our products conform to market guidelines and undergo rigorous testing prior to final delivery. As an ISO 9001-2015 certified company, we direct all our activities to cater to customer expectations by providing excellent quality products. We follow moral business policies and maintain crystal-pure transparency in all our transactions to keep healthy relations with our customers. For our accomplishment story, we are grateful to Owner Mr. Varun Shighal, whose continual backing and direction have been instrumental in attaining exponential development in the current market.
